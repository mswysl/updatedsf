**AnnouncementBar** — full-width strip at the top of the store (shipping, drop deadlines). Static or infinite `marquee`.

```jsx
<AnnouncementBar variant="pink">Free shipping over $75 · worldwide</AnnouncementBar>
<AnnouncementBar variant="lime" marquee items={['New drop: Friday 6pm', 'Strictly limited', 'No restocks']} />
```

Variants: `pink` · `lime` · `ink`.
